Performance Foodservice Northcenter

from Oct. 2014 - Present

Job Responsibilities:

  • offload returned product from trucks
  • ensure the emptied truck is ready for loading for next day's delivery by organizing load bars, load straps, temperature bulkheads, and pallets
  • check returned product for quality issues including temperature, damage, or spoilage
  • check in returns and ensure the "reason code" for the retuned product is appropriate and correct
  • ensure the return slot on the returned product is correct
  • perform inventory counts and adjustments
  • in any downtime assist with issuing credit to customers and filing credit, inventory, and return paperwork
  • work in a safe and respectful manner in a busy area of the warehouse with limited space and time
  • maintain a clean work area

As stated above, my main responsibilities currently with this job are to unload PFG's (Performance Foodgroup's) trucks as they return to the warehouse and check the quality, temperature, use by dates, and reason for return of any product that the customers send back with the truck drivers. In the small breaks I get in between those trucks I help out with processing credit requests for customers and I occasionaly help out with inventory control duties such as counting inventory, dealing with spoilage, transfering product into the freezer, or preparing products for donation.
